Instructions

How to use CS:GO Viz

1. Choose a CSGO map
2. Play around with the filters


Reset Filter: Resets all data points to default.

Counter-Terrorist: Apply filter for Counter-Terrorist only.

Terrorist: Apply filter for Terrorist only.

Where to camp with AWP?!: Density-based clustering on positional data of shooters using AWP.

Density Cluster: Density-based clustering on positional data of damage taken.

CT or T map?: Change bar chart to show which team has the most wins.

Best site to plant bomb?: Change bar chart to show the most popular bomb site.
